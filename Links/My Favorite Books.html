My Favorite Books

A Curated Bookshelf

A collection of books that have inspired, educated, and entertained me throughout my journey.

The Design of Everyday Things

by Don Norman

A powerful primer on how—and why—some products satisfy customers while others frustrate them.

Don't Make Me Think

by Steve Krug

A common-sense approach to Web usability. It's short, profusely illustrated—and best of all—fun to read.

Sprint

by Jake Knapp

How to Solve Big Problems and Test New Ideas in Just Five Days.

Invisible Cities

by Italo Calvino

A lyrical journey through imaginary cities, each more fantastical than the last.

The Little Prince

by Antoine de Saint-Exupéry

A timeless tale of love, loss, and the importance of seeing with the heart.

The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

Between life and death there is a library, and within that library, the shelves go on forever.
